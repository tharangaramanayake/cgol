Message From The President

As varied as our land, Sri Lankans are a piquant mix of many ethnic and cultural roots from these stem diverse cuisines.

For countless centuries we have continued to delight our visitors, and is no uncertain terms. The Chef’s Guild of Lanka makes its contribution towards the gastronomical satisfaction of our own nation and these visitors by introducing many new culinary facets and trends.

After forming the chef’s guild of Lanka in year 1988, Chefs in Sri Lanka earn worldwide recognition for their skills and talents. When the Sri Lankan team won the culinary Olympics 12’ the reputation for Sri Lankan chefs were recognized further by the culinary world with lots of honor to our small island.

The vision of Chefs guild;
To develop the talents of your chefs and to bring them forward to the international arena. To achieve this goal we involve the chefs in many training programs, cross exposure, competitions … etc for the culinary industry.

Culinary art 2013 will create more and more live cooking events than the previous competitions with the sponsorship power of our valuable food service partners at this event.

I take this opportunity to thank our

Last but not least a BIG THANK YOU! To the working committee of the Chefs Guild who have strived hard to make this even a success.

Chef Tyrell Wasalathanthrie
