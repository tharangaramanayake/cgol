Message From The Director

Designated as the director of this Culinary festival, I feel that this is “THE EVENT” of the year, where we, the seniors, the veterans get the opportunity to keep the young Sri Lankans venturing into the world of cookery to gain recognition, appreciation and ultimate culinary triumph.

Varied cuisine, scintillating Display, ambience, pleasing the eye proceeding, tasting, all this contribute to the enjoyment of food. We pushed into the background of the idea “food for survival”. For us advert in the art of cooking is in food for relishing. It is an accepted notice that the way to a person’s heart is through his stomach. A contented stomach means a happy heart, a pleasant mood. This wonder of rapture and salvation is created through culinary skills

Competition leads to innovation which eventually helps to surface new techniques and new experiments captivating the hearts and minds of individuals enjoying food.

It is culinary skills that promote tourism to a great extent. Each year entries keep on increasing and this time it has amplified to 1586! We can expect novelty, beauty and high achievements in all areas of food and beverages thus paving the way for culinary excellence.

So together with the Chef’s Guild of Lanka let me welcome you to the greatest Food & Hospitality show CULINARY ART 2013

Sunil Edirisinghe
Director
