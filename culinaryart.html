Culinary Art








Culinary art is the art of cooking. Culinary artists are responsible for skillfully preparing meals that are as pleasing to the palate as to the eye. Increasingly they are required to possess a knowledge of the science of food and an understanding of diet and nutrition.

Culinary Art Competitions and Exhibitions not only play a vital role in culinary arts as they continually raise the standards of culinary excellence, but there is no better way for a culinarian to their craft than by putting their skills and knowledge to the test in a competitive format.

Culinary Art exhibitions are conducted to provide models, as well as a podium for the international development of the culinary art itself. Where else but at these such exhibitions have the public and the professional world exposed to state of the art culinary creations and learning experiences which promote acceptance of new trends, techniques and philosophies???

Not everyone has the resources to traverse the world to witness and sample new developments and exotic creations from alien cultures. Here at Culinary Art, we bring the world to their feet.

Apart from the displays of world wide cuisines and trends, the “New Sri Lankan Cuisine” competition entices the participating chefs to experiment with local foods and to innovative new and attractive presentations taking into consideration its nutritive properties. This continually improves the standard of Sri Lanka Cuisine which in turn builds a stronger tourism product within the Asian region.

Thus the importance of “Culinary Art” and it’s effect and purpose.
