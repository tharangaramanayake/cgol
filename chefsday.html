International Chefs Day









It‘s not often that one sees a Chef in his uniform outside of the kitchen! But that is exactly where 250 chefs from different parts of the Island were on the evening of 20 November 2012 at Cinnamon Bey Beruwala. International Chefs Day has been marked as a special occasion by culinirians to solute the profession and creates awareness of Chefs who have set remarkable industry standards for the art of cooking and who infuse passion into the food industry throughout the world. This commemorative day was initiated by the World Association of Cooks Societies (WACS), which counts over eight million Chefs from all over the world as members.

The 18th International Chefs Day, sponsored by Fonterra Brands Lanka, was celebrated here in Beruwala amongst the beautiful setting at the cinnamon bey, with an astonishing view of the serene ocean. This exclusive 18th Anniversary celebration began with the lighting of a followed by a number of enticing dances that left our chefs glued to their seats with “eyes wide open!”

In gratitude of the patronage received by Fonterra Brands Lanka over the past 15 years, the Chefs Guild of Lanka presented plaques to All senior management at the Fonterra Brand Lanka.

Breaking away from the usual day outing, this year′s event went on with the Chefs dancing away to the tunes of Ronnie Leach and also Chef Don Sherman, our “singing chef” who kept the crowd well entertained and also proved that chefs could be multi–talented folk!!

As the saying goes: “all good things must come to an end” and International Chefs Day 2012 certainly ended on a very “high” note!!
